About

Established in the fall of 2005 and already few weeks later this distinct coffee house became one of the city's most popular meeting venues. Until nowadays, its authentic interiors are not only one of first choices for local citizens, but also for tourist, who seek for authentic experiences.It is a sheer delight to sit at The Polish Cafe - relax, read the newspaper and have the best cappuccino in town or a fresh fruit juice. Overlooking a secluded skating park in the heart of the community , this is a charming place to go out for breakfast, lunch, or just for a delicious slice of cake.
